# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **security@aipowergrid.io** or open a private GitHub security advisory.
Do not open public issues for security reports.

- We acknowledge reports within 72 hours and aim to triage within 7 days.
- Give us a reasonable coordinated-disclosure window to ship a fix before
  publishing details.
- We credit reporters who follow this process.

## Scope

In scope: this repository's code, smart contracts, deployment and registration
tooling, and protocol handling.

Out of scope: third-party dependencies (report those upstream), social
engineering, and volumetric denial of service.

## Smart contracts

On-chain code on Base is verified and public. Findings affecting funds, access
control, provenance, deployment integrity, or settlement receive the highest
priority. Include the contract address, chain ID, affected source revision, a
minimal proof of concept, and likely impact when available.
